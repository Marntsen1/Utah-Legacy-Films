Please save your 3 images to the `public` folder with these exact names:

1. **logo.png** - Your Utah Legacy Films logo (tree stump with quill pen)

2. **interview-setup.jpg** (or .png) - The professional video interview setup image

3. **family-watching.jpg** (or .png) - The family watching TV image
